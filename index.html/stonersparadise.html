Business Concepts

Cafe

The idea of the cafe is to be able to provide both edibles and liquids infused with THC at the highest quality and lowest prices possible, we want people to feel like the dispensary is inviting and not just a business transaction. We want to help you meet new people and build good realationships through the experience of our products and make you want to come back for more, we are more than just a normal dispensary.

Show Room

The show room will allow people to get first person access to the actual operation of the business. It allows us to build a sense of trust and gives the customer the satisfaction of knowing how their product is grown and accumulated. It's also a pretty exilerating experience to see such an overwhelming amount and the details of how it's done.

Dispensary

We want to have a sleek modern look to the dispensary while also not appearing to be an unafforadble high end store. We want products on display and at a reasonable price. We plan to sell each and every way and type of smoking at the store at the best price around. We will include both local and exotic strains and have more than enough options for you to choose from each time you visit.
